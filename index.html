Andong Yang

Hi there! I am a PhD student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I enjoy assembling new robots and studying algorithms to make robots more powerful. My research focuses on bringing robots to work in challenging environments. These days, I'm interested in training neural networks to allocate tasks for a heterogeneous multi-robot system.


Andong Yang is a PhD student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where he studying on motion planning and control in robotics. He is interested in building machines and algorithms that enable robots to intelligently operate in chenllenging environments. Andong has published papers in leading robotics conferences and journals, including IROS, ICRA, and RA-L. He has also served as a reviewer for ICRA and IROS, and has been awarded the Academic First Class Scholarship.
